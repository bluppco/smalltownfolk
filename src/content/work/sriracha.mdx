        ## Making an impression 
        
       We were very inspired by the authenticity of the recipes that Sriracha used. When creating the visuals for this project, we went with a simplified take on wood cutting illustrations. Originally, these illustrations were created on blocks of wood and used a maximum of three different colours. We played with outlines and solid fills to create distinctive shapes, reminiscent of this style. Using these basic principles to create food illustrations and eventually, outdoor posters.

</Column>
